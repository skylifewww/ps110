Image Upload

Please upload a minimum of 3-5 product shots of the same bag along with lifestyle picture of your piece worn or shot in a way to represent proportions.Great shots will encourage oureditors to use your products when postings stories so try to stick to the following guidelines:

  • Only upload JPG or PNG formats no larger than 4mb each.
  • High Resolution
  • No logos or watermarks as those images will be rejected.
  • Please use a clean white background for all"Product Shots."This means,no hangers,flowers,pets or any other items that you think will make your bag look better.It won't.
  • Stuffed bags give life to the body shape and unstuffed bags look flat and not worth purchasing.
  • Product Shots consist of different views of your bag to show the customer all facets of your bag including side view and interior view.
  • Make sure your bag is displayed in the best possible light and presentation possible.
  • No black border or decoration on any of the images as those will be rejected.
  • For"Lifestyle Shots"please use a person and be thoughtful of how the bag is held.Make sure your bag is the focal point of the shot.
  • Please no nudity,bikini or racy photos of your models holding your bags.Those photos will be rejected.
